A Multi-Faceted Approach

One partner for every step of the revenue cycle

The path to a streamlined revenue cycle starts with a complete evaluation — we analyze processes, performance, unpaid claims, collection efforts and financial data to understand where you stand and where the opportunities are. Then we partner with you across patient access, coding, billing, collections, denial management and analytics.

Medical Billing & Coding

Certified, experienced coders produce accurate, compliant claims and faster, cleaner submissions across every specialty — reducing errors that cost you revenue.

Denial Management

Reduce denials and errors with support that stays ahead of changing payer reimbursement rules — and resolves rejections quickly to protect your cash flow.

AR Follow-Up & Recovery

Dedicated teams pursue aging accounts and legacy AR backlogs across time zones to convert more receivables into collected cash.

Charge Entry & Payment Posting

Timely, accurate charge capture and payment posting keep your revenue cycle flowing — with reconciliation that eliminates leakage.

Patient Access & Pre-Registration

Patient Collections

Coding Compliance & Auditing

Credentialing & Enrollment

Real-time dashboards, trends, competitive analysis and business intelligence so you can benchmark, forecast and grow with confidence.
